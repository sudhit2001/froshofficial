About
Us

Frosh is an alliance of students formed for the freshers whose incentive is to accompany them in the smooth transitioning from school to college. From guiding them through the admission process, organizing sensational events, exciting games, multitude of astonishing workshops followed by guest lectures, Frosh allows freshmen to acknowledge and enhance their skills, interests and talents. This helps individuals to build their own identity at a new place which makes them fearless and self-confident.

Our Team

A productive environment is created by carrying out these duties with the utmost energy and motivation each day resulting in the formation of a close-knit team. A team always succeeds in reaching its goal when all members work in cooperation to complete their allocated tasks.
By planning several events for the freshmen's acclimatization to the new environment, including both technical and non-technical skills, FROSH 2022, a team of 70 students, hopes to help the freshmen make a smooth transition from their school life to the college habits.
